About BPM International School

Shahpur, Sirdala, Nawada

Established in 2011, BPM International School is committed to providing high-quality education in an environment that promotes growth, inclusivity, and excellence. Education goes beyond academic marks—it nurtures good values, critical thinking, and a vision to create a better world. Our mission is to develop responsible and capable individuals for the future.

Our Approach

We focus on holistic development, addressing intellectual, social, emotional, physical, artistic, and spiritual growth. Our aim is not only academic excellence but also fostering independent thinking and a love for learning that will benefit students throughout their lives.

Our Vision

We aspire to create confident, kind, and respectful individuals who are ready to face challenges with courage. Our value-based education system instills life skills and discipline, empowering students to succeed in their chosen fields.

Beyond Academics

A strong foundation in the early years is crucial for a child’s overall development. We emphasize a well-rounded education that extends beyond textbooks. Active participation in co-curricular activities fuels curiosity, builds character, and helps students unlock their potential. Our experienced faculty is dedicated to guiding each student toward excellence.
