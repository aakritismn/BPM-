About BPM International School

BPM International School, Shahpur, Sirdala, Nawada is dedicated to providing high-quality education with a vision to empower students with knowledge, values, and discipline. Our school follows a holistic approach to nurture young minds into responsible and skilled individuals.

We focus not just on academic excellence but also on character-building, leadership, and critical thinking. Our experienced faculty ensures that students develop intellectual, emotional, and social skills to face real-world challenges with confidence.

With a strong foundation in co-curricular activities, sports, and modern education techniques, BPM International School aims to create a dynamic learning environment where students grow with creativity, innovation, and self-discipline.

Our Facilities

BPM International School Shahpur Sirdala Nawada has a highly-equipped campus to facilitate the holistic growth of our students, beyond the classrooms. Keeping up with modern technology via digital classrooms, subject-wise laboratories, studio rooms, canteen, sports complexes and other amenities, we are equipped to make learning fun and engaging.
