CBSE Affiliation

Affiliation Number - 330552

From primary school through senior high school, BPM International School, Shahpur Sirdala, Nawada has established curriculum, instruction, and evaluation criteria. Exams for classes X and XII are conducted. As the school is CBSE affiliated, we have a strong curriculum, excellent teaching, stress-free assessments, and comprehensive learning. The school strictly follows the CBSE program starting in primary school.

This ensures that a child's education remains consistent and of a high standard throughout their school years. The only variation is in the way the data is analysed. The CBSE places a lot of emphasis on the school's overall environment. Affiliation with the CBSE essentially means preparing students for the Board's Secondary and Senior Secondary examinations according to prescribed courses.
